# Auvic Solutions - User Manual

## 📘 Introduction
Welcome to **Auvic Solutions**, your comprehensive platform for managing business operations, project lifecycles, and financial workflows. This manual provides a step-by-step guide on how to navigate and utilize the software effectively.

---

## 🔐 1. Accessing the Application

### 1.1 Login
To access your workspace:
1. Navigate to the application URL.
2. Enter your registered **Email Address**.
3. Enter your **Password**.
4. Click **Sign In**.

![Login Screen](/assets/manual/login.png)

### 1.2 Registration
If you don't have an account:
1. Click **"register your company"** on the login page.
2. Provide your Company Name, Full Name, Email, and a Secure Password.
3. Once registered, you will be logged in automatically.

---

## 📊 2. Dashboard & Analytics
The **Dashboard** is the first screen you'll see. It provides:
- **Project Pulse**: Real-time count of active vs. completed jobs.
- **Financial Insights**: Total revenue projections based on ongoing jobs.
- **Efficiency Metrics**: Total hours logged.

![Dashboard Preview](/assets/manual/dashboard.png)

---

## 🛠️ 3. Managing Your Business

### 3.1 Job Board (Kanban)
The **Jobs** tab uses a visual pipeline. 
- **Creating a Job**: Click the blue `+ New Job` button.
- **Tracking Progress**: View jobs categorized by their current stage (Incoming, Estimation, In Progress, etc.).
- **Detailed View**: Click any job card to open the **Job Detail View**, where you can manage timers, logs, and files.

![Job Board](/assets/manual/jobs.png)

### 3.2 Clients
The **Clients** tab maintains your customer database. Clients are automatically added when you create a job for a new customer, or you can manage them manually.

![Clients Management](/assets/manual/clients.png)

### 3.3 Invoicing & Payments
Generate and track invoices based on job completion.
1. Navigate to **Invoices**.
2. View pending and paid amounts.
3. Send secure payment links to customers.

![Invoicing](/assets/manual/invoices.png)

---

## ⚙️ 4. Configuration & Security

### 4.1 Business Settings
Upload your logo and define your business details in the **Settings** tab. This information is used for branding your portal and invoices.

![Business Settings](/assets/manual/settings.png)

### 4.2 Security & 2FA
Add an extra layer of protection:
1. Go to **My Profile**.
2. Toggle **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**.
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app.

![Profile & Security](/assets/manual/profile.png)

---

## ❓ 5. Help & Search
Need quick answers?
- Use the **Search Bar** in the header to find jobs, clients, or specific settings.
- Click **Help Center** in the sidebar to access our searchable, step-by-step FAQ and guides.
